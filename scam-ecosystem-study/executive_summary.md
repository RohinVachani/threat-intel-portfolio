# Executive Summary

This casefile analyses why Indian scam networks scale rapidly, the actors who 
participate, the operational structures they adopt, and the psychological levers they 
exploit. The document is organized as 15 focused analytical sections (behavioral, 
operational, and strategic) that together form a cohesive picture of how scams propagate, 
escalate, and collapse.

Key findings (short):
- Scams scale due to large victim density, low enforcement risk, and low technical 
barriers.
- Networks rely on role specialization, tech operators, and mule flows to preserve 
anonymity and increase throughput.

manipulation beats technical sophistication.
